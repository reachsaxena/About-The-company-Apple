About Apple

Apple is one of the most popular tech company. Apple started on April 1, 1976 by
Steve Jobs and Steve Wozniak. Steve Jobs and Steve Wozniak wanted to make computers
small enough for people to have them in their home or offices.

Definition of Apple

A company that makes things like computers, phones, iPads and watches

How dose Apple relate to the world

In the 2019 around 1.4 billion Apple products were being used.

The history of Apple

Apple Computers, Inc. was founded on April 1, 1976, by college dropouts
Steve Jobs and Steve Wozniak, who brought to the new company a vision of
changing the way people viewed computers. One more thing that changed
over time is their logo. The first logo looked like a Apple fall on newton's head.
And the most recent one looks like a gray Apple.

Fun Facts about Apple

1. The Apple I computer went on sale in july 1976 at a price of 666.66
US-Dollar.
2. During his time as CEO of Apple Steve Jobs only received a fixed salary of
one US-Dollar per year. However due to his shares in Apple his net
worth was estimated at 10.2 billion US-Dollar at the time of his death in 2011.
3.In 1986, Apple launched a clothing line but with no success.
The collection included shirts, pants and sunglasses but also other things
like a watch, a chair or even a sailboard.
